Scene One

Our Story

Every great love story deserves its own opening credits. Here's how ours unfolded — frame by frame, moment by moment, until it became forever.

2019

How We Met

A mutual friend's mehndi, a crowded dance floor, and one accidental step on the wrong foot — quite literally. Hassan apologised three times; Ayesha laughed every time. Neither of them remembers the song that was playing, but they both remember exactly how it felt.

2019

The First Conversation

A borrowed phone number "just in case the photos turned out blurry" turned into a three-hour conversation about old Lahore, favourite qawwals, and the best karachi karahi in town. The photos, by the way, were never sent.

2020

The First Date

Coffee at a quiet café on MM Alam Road turned into a four-hour walk that neither of them planned. They talked about everything and nothing — and somewhere between the second cup of chai and the sunset, they both quietly knew.

2022

Meeting the Families

Sunday lunches became a tradition — first with Ayesha's family, then Hassan's. What started as polite formality soon became loud laughter, friendly arguments over cricket, and the unmistakable feeling of two families slowly becoming one.

2025

The Proposal

Under the fairy lights of their favourite rooftop, with old film songs playing softly in the background, Hassan got down on one knee. Ayesha said yes before he could finish the sentence — and the night sky over Lahore had never looked more golden.

2026

Journey to Forever

And now, surrounded by the people we love most, our story moves into its next chapter. Thank you for being part of it from the very beginning — and for joining us as the credits roll on "single" and the title card reads "happily ever after."

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear to each event?+

Each event has a suggested dress code listed in the Wedding Events section above — but most importantly, come comfortable and ready to celebrate!

Are children welcome?+

Absolutely! All our little guests are welcome at every event. Please include them in your guest count when you RSVP.

Is parking available at the venues?+

Yes, complimentary valet parking will be available at Royal Palm, Pearl Continental, and Avari Hotel for all wedding events.

Can I bring a plus-one?+

Please refer to your personalised invitation for the number of guests reserved under your name, and reflect this in your RSVP.

Will there be food for special diets?+

Yes — vegetarian, vegan and gluten-free options will be available at every event. Please mention any dietary requirements in your RSVP.
